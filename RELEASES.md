# Releases

## v0.1.0-beta — Public Beta

**Released:** May 2026

The first public release of Geofancy. The chart engine, perfection analyzer, Way of Points module, and original interpretive corpus are all production-ready; the surrounding app continues to evolve.

### Highlights

- Live deployment at <https://geofancy.up.railway.app>
- Full responsive workspace with separate desktop and mobile layouts; mobile devices are auto-routed
- Original interpretive corpus for all sixteen figures, twelve houses, and four court positions, written from primary sources and authored entirely by the project owner
- Themed card-based detail panel: contextual "this figure in this slot" callout, split favorable / unfavorable cards, person and body correspondences, house affinity pills, and citation footer
- Perfection analyzer with Querent / Quesited selection and per-perfection commentary
- Way of Points analyzer with path-type explanations and structured tips
- Light and dark modes
- Source-available under the PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0 license; corpus separately under CC BY-NC 4.0

### Known limitations

- Charts are not yet persisted across sessions; refreshing the page resets the workspace.
- The legacy WinForms desktop app remains available for offline use but does not yet reflect the full corpus rewrite or the new card layout. Web is the recommended surface.
- The Way of Points and Aspect Analysis pages are marked experimental in places where their interpretive layer is still being expanded.
